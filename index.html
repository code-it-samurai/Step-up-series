Items Included In 10kg Dumbbell Pair System

1. High quality chrome plated steel handles(2)
2. locking screws (4)
3. Black powdercoated weight plates which weight 1.25kg(6)


HOW TO USE
Step 1: first take take your chrome plated steel handle with attached plates on its sides

Step 2: add plate A on the first(smallest) step of locking screw and screw it in the steel handle

Step 3: screw equal amount of weight on both sides

Step 4: to increase the weight, remove screws from steel handle, remove plate A from screw and add plate B to the second(middle) step of screw, then add plate A after it on first(smallest) step of screw and insert the screw in steel handle, now you have doubled the weight

Step 5: to add a third plate to your dumbbell, remove the screw and plates, and this time insert plate C first in the screw, on the first step(largest), and then add plate B and plate A, now you have maxed weight on your dumbbell
